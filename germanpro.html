Main Insightsüí¨
  • Motivations: Cultural exploration, personal connections, and career growth.
  • Methods: Conversational practice, immersion, reading, apps, and structured courses.
  • Apps for Beginners: Popular apps are great for beginners but lack features for advanced learners, like AI-driven conversational practice.
  • Apps vs Real-World Fluency: Apps complement traditional methods but are insufficient for achieving real-world fluency.
