My Journey

My adventure began in the vibrant highlands of Quito, stretched through the dynamic pulse of New York, and thrived in Berlin's innovative heart.

  • Starting with a pursuit of social justice through law in Quito, I quickly uncovered a knack for clear communication and sharp analysis. Yet, it was the magnetic charm of business development that truly captured my spirit for innovation.
  • A pivotal six months in New York set my soul ablaze with a love for art and photography, carving a path I never anticipated.
  • Berlin was the next chapter, where the world of user-centered design unfolded before me, culminating in an MBA that would redefine my career.

Each city painted a unique stroke on my professional canvas, guiding me through varied roles and projects.

The leap from Law to Product Design was bold, marrying my passions for creativity, meticulous research, and solving puzzles in ways I had only dreamed of.

Today, I dedicate myself to projects that resonate deeply with my core values: championing environmental preservation, human rights, access to legal services, and the transformative power of education.
