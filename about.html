From Law to UX?

Transitioning from Law to UX Design may seem unconventional, but it was driven by a strong passion for research, creativity, and problem-solving. This journey reflects my shift from empathetic listening and understanding to devising innovative solutions that align business objectives with user needs. In User Experience Design (UX), I found my true calling.

With a background as a Digital Strategist and Legal Operations Manager, I bring a solid track record in effective team and project management. My expertise in User-Centered Design is enriched by a deep knowledge of the legal services sector, showcasing strong problem-solving and analytical skills honed through rigorous research and a dedication to ongoing improvement.

My objective is to engage in meaningful and impactful projects, ideally with organizations or individuals committed to environmental conservation, human rights, education, or legal services.
