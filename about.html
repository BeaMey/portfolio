From Law to UX?

Transitioning from Law to UX Design may seem unconventional, but it was fueled by my natural problem-solving abilities, curiosity, and innovation drive.

My legal background honed my analytical skills, research capabilities and dedication to service, but the frustration of legal outcomes tied to political influences or arbitral decisions pushed me to explore other business facets like legal marketing, content creation and other business development strategies. This journey not only revealed my aptitude for these areas but also my craving for creativity.

In User Experience Design (UX), I found my true calling, where empathy and problem-solving align.
